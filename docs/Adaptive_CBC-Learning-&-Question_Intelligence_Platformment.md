# **Product Vision & Engineering Foundation** 

# **1\. Executive Overview**

The Adaptive CBC Learning & Question Intelligence Platform is an AI-powered educational ecosystem designed to transform how students in Kenya learn, revise, practice, and prepare for examinations under the CBC curriculum.

The platform combines:

* intelligent question banks,  
* AI-assisted learning,  
* adaptive revision systems,  
* OCR digitization of past papers,  
* tutor marketplaces,  
* gamified education,  
* and curriculum-aware analytics

into a unified learning environment accessible via web and mobile applications.

Unlike traditional revision books and static question bank systems, the platform continuously evolves by:

* digitizing examination papers,  
* understanding educational content using AI,  
* generating new question variations,  
* tracking learner performance,  
* and adapting revision experiences to each student’s learning level and weaknesses.

The system is designed for:

* Grades 1–9 initially,  
* with future expansion into Senior Secondary School.

Its long-term vision is to become:

# **the educational intelligence infrastructure for CBC learning.**

---

# **2\. The Problem Being Solved**

## **Current Educational Challenges**

Students today face several limitations:

* Revision books become exhausted after repeated use.  
* Questions are static and finite.  
* Learning is not personalized.  
* Students cram instead of mastering competencies.  
* Teachers spend excessive time preparing assessments.  
* Parents cannot effectively monitor learning progress.  
* Schools lack intelligent analytics about student performance.

Additionally:

* past papers exist mostly in non-searchable scanned formats,  
* educational resources are fragmented,  
* and students often struggle to identify weak areas early enough.

---

# **5\. Major Platform Components**

## **A. Intelligent Question Bank**

Central repository containing:

* multiple choice questions,  
* open-ended questions,  
* mathematical problems,  
* scientific diagrams,  
* comprehension passages,  
* structured answers,  
* marking schemes,  
* competency tags,  
* topic mapping,  
* difficulty ratings,  
* and question variants.

The system supports:

* curriculum alignment,  
* semantic search,  
* and adaptive question recommendation.

---

## **B. OCR & Educational Content Digitization**

The platform allows:

* scanned paper uploads,  
* image uploads,  
* PDF uploads,  
* and camera captures.

AI then:

* extracts text,  
* detects mathematical formulas,  
* identifies diagrams,  
* segments questions,  
* recognizes answer options,  
* and structures the content into digital educational records.

This creates:

* searchable educational archives,  
* reusable question intelligence,  
* and scalable educational datasets.

---

## **C. Adaptive Learning Engine**

The adaptive engine:

* tracks student performance,  
* identifies weaknesses,  
* predicts mastery levels,  
* and adjusts revision difficulty dynamically.

Students receive:

* personalized revision plans,  
* targeted practice,  
* and spaced repetition learning.

---

## **D. AI Learning Assistant**

The AI tutor:

* explains solutions step-by-step,  
* generates similar questions,  
* simplifies concepts,  
* provides hints,  
* and adapts explanations to learner level.

Future enhancements may include:

* multilingual support,  
* voice interaction,  
* and visual teaching assistance.

---

## **E. Tutor & Teacher Marketplace**

Educators can:

* register as tutors,  
* host live classes,  
* upload learning materials,  
* submit questions,  
* create assignments,  
* and monetize educational services.

Students can:

* book tutors,  
* join discussion groups,  
* and attend revision sessions.

---

## **F. Gamified Learning Environment**

To reduce revision fatigue, the platform incorporates:

* badges,  
* XP points,  
* streaks,  
* learning leagues,  
* timed challenges,  
* leaderboards,  
* and educational mini-games.

The goal is:

# **sustained learning engagement.**

---

## **G. Smart Educational Search**

Students and teachers can search using:

* keywords,  
* competencies,  
* difficulty,  
* curriculum strands,  
* semantic meaning,  
* and question similarity.

Examples:

* “Grade 9 algebra with diagrams”  
* “CBC ratio questions”  
* “questions similar to KCPE perimeter”

---

## **H. Analytics & Performance Tracking**

The platform tracks:

* strengths,  
* weaknesses,  
* revision consistency,  
* competency growth,  
* learning pace,  
* and predicted performance.

This helps:

* students,  
* parents,  
* teachers,  
* and schools.
